About


Kassidy is a self-taught illustrator & designer with a love for storytelling, colorful characters, and the natural environment. When she's not seated at her work desk, she's always searching for something new to spark her creativity.


She has also worked with over 20 partners from 2018-2024, which includes individual clients and local businesses. Her ultimate goal is to provide the tools entrepreneurs need to market themselves, and to deliver artistic product(s) to be enjoyed for years to come.
