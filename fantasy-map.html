Fantasy Map

Objective: Creating an interactive fictional world map.

The Method

This interactive map is a personal venture that I've converted into additional prototyping practice, which is of course an essential skill to have as a web designer. I consider it my most extensive Adobe XD project.

The map features 40 total buttons that toggle a respective "description" window. A live preview can be found here (best viewed on desktop).

Following the medieval setting of this fictional world, the visuals would take on an elegant fantasy style as well. Users are encouraged to interact with various buttons in order to explore different locales. Upon clicking a locale name, its description and a scenic image representing that specific territory are shown. This new window may be closed with another click.

The Lesson

While designing this project, I was forced to view the experience from a user's perspective and solve problems accordingly. Questions such as "Is the design convenient?" and "Could the directions be simplified or improved in any way?" compelled me to develop a user-friendly interface, and have also made me confident in my ability to design online user experiences.
