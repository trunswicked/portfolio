Alesco Logos

Objective: Creating a series of logos for a local landscaping firm.

The Method

Alesco Design Solutions is a landscaping firm based in Phoenix, Arizona that specializes in environmental planning, placemaking and aesthetics, and sustainability. The business also prides itself in a local presence, and with this in mind, I set out to craft the logo that would best represent their values.

It is pertinent to first establish where a logo will appear. In the case of Alesco, the design is used in signage, printed documents, sticker sheets, and on a website, indicating that the logos should be exported in PNG and SVG format to accommodate these mediums. The main logo takes on a rectangular shape, but several variants of the final design also appear on a 4 x 6 inch sticker sheet.

The Lesson

This branding venture is one of the first design projects I have ever completed in a professional setting. I'm accustomed to contacting clients via social media and email, but this project allowed me to meet with a client face-to-face and develop a personal understanding of their needs. It was an excellent, real-world experience!
